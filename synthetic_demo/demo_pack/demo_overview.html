6) XML delivery checkpoint

The flow reaches final-stage XML validation. Current constraint is a workbook mandatory-field rule, identified at the final delivery gate.

7) Architecture summary

One lender tape is ingested once and transformed into a canonical truth set. From that single controlled source, validation, MI, and Annex 2 outputs are produced consistently. This gives a clear audit trail from raw submission to reporting-ready deliverables.
